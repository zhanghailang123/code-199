## 核心概念

**Cut** 搭配不同介词/副词，意义差异很大。

## 常见搭配

| 短语 | 含义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| **cut down** | 削减、砍倒 | cut down costs 削减成本 |
| **cut off** | 切断、中断 | cut off the water supply 切断供水 |
| **cut out** | 删除、停止 | cut out junk food 戒掉垃圾食品 |
| **cut in** | 插嘴、插入 | Don't cut in! 别插嘴！ |
| **cut back** | 减少、削减 | cut back on spending 减少开支 |
| **cut through** | 穿过、克服 | cut through the bureaucracy 打破官僚作风 |

## 高频搭配（考试常见）
- cut down on expenses / costs / sugar
- cut off electricity / communication
- cut out the middleman

## 易错点
- cut down vs cut back：基本同义，但 cut back 更常用于预算/开支
- cut off vs cut out：cut off 强调中断，cut out 强调删除/去掉
